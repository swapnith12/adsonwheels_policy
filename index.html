Privacy Policy for Adsonwheels

At Adsonwheels, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: When you sign up for an account, we collect your email address and name.
  • Location Data: The app continuously collects and stores your location to deliver location-based advertisements and measure the distance traveled.
  • Face Detection Data: We use face detection technology to identify different passengers for personalized ad experiences. This data is not stored permanently.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about the device you use to access the app, such as the device's model, operating system, and IP address.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • Delivering personalized advertisements based on your location and preferences.
  • Measuring the effectiveness of advertisements by analyzing face detection and location data.
  • Improving the app’s functionality and user experience.
  • Ensuring compliance with legal obligations and protecting against fraud and abuse.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Advertising partners to deliver targeted ads.
  • Service providers who assist us in app development, analytics, or customer support.
  • Law enforcement or regulatory agencies if required by law or in response to legal processes.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please note that no method of electronic transmission is completely secure.

5. Your Choices

You have the following choices regarding your information:

  • You may choose to disable location tracking in your device settings, but this may affect your ability to receive personalized advertisements.
  • You can request access to or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at support@adsonwheels.com.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you via the app or by email. Please review this policy regularly to stay informed about how we protect your data.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at support@adsonwheels.com.
